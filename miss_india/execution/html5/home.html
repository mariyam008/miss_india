ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Height: 5’5″ and above

Age: 18-25 Years (25 Years as of 31st December 2018).

Single, Unmarried and Not Engaged.

Indian Passport Holder/Overseas Indian Citizen.

26 & 27 years old can only compete for the Runner-up positions as the eligibility for Miss World is 18-25.

OCI cardholders cannot be the winner of Miss India. But can only be the first or second runner-up.

The participant required to carry a passport, birth certificate, school leaving certificate, driver license for age and birth proof.
